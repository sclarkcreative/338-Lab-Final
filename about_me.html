the scullery syd story

Hi! I'm Sydney. I basically grew up in the kitchen and love everything that has to do with food. I am a third year student at Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo. I love creating things, whether it be a recipe or a beautifully designed brochure. Being a Graphic Communication student, I have always loved design. I always try to combine these two loves in a unique way. This past October, I started working as a culinary instructor at Williams-Sonoma, Inc. I teach both kids and adults how to cook...some of my favorite classes have been “Cooking in Cast Iron” “Knife Skills” and “American Girl Birthday Party.”
